About Me

Anukriti, an experienced Data Professional, has a proven track record of delivering impactful solutions and fostering innovation within the Healthcare, Banking, and Supply Chain sectors. Her previous roles with esteemed organizations such as UnitedHealth Group, JnJ, Amex, and HSBC underscore her breadth of experience. Proficient in technical domains including ML, NLP, Deep Learning, and Graph Learning, Anukriti has spearheaded the development of cutting-edge algorithms and models across various industries. These initiatives have consistently led to substantial cost savings and process improvements for her clients. Her adeptness in team leadership and client relationship management has been pivotal in surpassing project expectations and delivering comprehensive solutions. Throughout her career journey, Anukriti has been honored with notable awards and recognition, including academic scholarships and commendations from distinguished figures such as Arun Jaitley and Shashi Tharoor. In her most recent role, she managed a diverse client portfolio across the Asia Pacific region, where she led the implementation of AI-driven solutions for banks, airlines, and other sectors. Anukriti's educational background includes a Master's degree in Mathematics from IIT Bombay, complemented by a LLB degree. This robust academic foundation harmonizes seamlessly with her wealth of practical experience.
